Questions Before Joining?
Why hire a Rora Coach?
Negotiating your own job offer can create an immense about of anxiety and self-doubt. It's a time pressured and critical decision making process that involves many personal and professional factors. It takes self-control and effective advocacy to build alignment and not damage relationships.
It's also incredibly awkward to negotiate with individuals that are supposed to be your future teammates. Instead of making negotiations personal and potentially relationship damaging, we make negotiations objective and protect the relationship.
Why pay for this?
The cheapest dollar to spend on your career is not later when you’ve failed; it’s to invest early and allow that investment to compound.
Effectively advocating for yourself in your career is the highest ROI skill on your time you can invest in.
Smarter negotiating can be the difference of hundreds of thousands of dollars and sets a floor for future compensation. Compound this over a 30 year career and it's obvious, better decisions have a 10X impact on your career.
How do you charge?
For your job search, we charge a fixed fee to prepare you for interviews.
For your offer negotiation, we charge as a % of the negotiated delta of the job offer we help you negotiate. No increase, no fee. Our company does not survive unless we impact your career.
For executive coaching, we charge a monthly fee.
Aren't you only short term incentive aligned?
No, because we understand the way to the highest compensation is through impact, experience, and career capital. We advise our clients to go where they can be the most impactful, money is a by-product of this.
When should I speak to you?
When you are starting to look for a new full time position. The strategy for your negotiation begins as soon as you reach out to a company. If you’re already negotiating we can still help, but please reach out ASAP since your need is time sensitive.
Who qualifies?
AI Researchers in machine learning, language models, computer vision, multimodal models, reinforcement learning, robotics, embodied AI, and AI for Science.
How do you work?
We work with you 1:1 to prepare you for interviews, set up mock interview loops, and coach you how to negotiate job offers in a way that does not damage relationships. Once employed we also offer coaching on how to manage up, build influence, and soft skills.
If I’m experienced at negotiating is it worth it?
We have helped professionals with 20+ years of experience negotiate better than they would on their own. We have done L9 offer negotiations worth tens of millions of dollars.
Can I lose my offer from negotiating?
Yes, it is possible. We have the data to know that it is exceedingly rare, less than 1% of the time. Your risk also depends on
how
you negotiate – we identify the signals when you are at risk, and manage that risk appropriately.
